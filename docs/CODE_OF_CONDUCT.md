# Code of Conduct

## Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Standards

### Positive
- Empathy and kindness
- Respectful disagreement
- Constructive feedback
- Accountability for mistakes
- Community-focused decisions

### Unacceptable
- Sexualized language or advances
- Trolling, insults, personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information
- Unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Leaders will enforce these standards. Reports to **conduct@chakravyuh.dev** are confidential and investigated promptly.

### Consequences

| Violation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Minor (inappropriate language) | Private warning |
| Single incident | Warning + no-contact period |
| Sustained behavior | Temporary ban |
| Pattern of violations | Permanent ban |

## Scope

All community spaces (GitHub, Discord, events) and public representations.
